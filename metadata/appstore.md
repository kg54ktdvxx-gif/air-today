## Description (4000 char max)

```
Air Today turns air quality into something you can feel. Instead of burying you in numbers, it paints the sky — the entire screen shifts from clear blue to hazy amber to deep red, so you know the moment you open the app whether it's a good day to be outside.

The air quality index is there if you want it. But the real answer is the verdict at the top of the screen: "Great day to be outside" or "Sensitive groups should limit time outdoors." Plain language, no interpretation required.

KNOW WHAT YOU CAN DO TODAY
Air Today gives you per-activity guidance for the things that actually matter:
• Running and cycling — how hard can you push?
• Walking and outdoor dining — is it pleasant out there?
• Playground time with kids — are the little ones safe?

Each recommendation is time-aware. Morning air is often different from afternoon air, and Air Today accounts for that.

SEE WHAT'S COMING
A 9-day air quality forecast, visualized with clean Swift Charts, helps you plan ahead. Going for a long run Saturday? Check Thursday. Planning a birthday party in the park? You'll know days in advance.

EXPLORE NEARBY STATIONS
A built-in map shows every monitoring station around you, each pinned with a color-coded AQI marker. Tap any pin to see that station's current reading. You might be surprised how much air quality varies across just a few miles.

TRACK MULTIPLE LOCATIONS
Add up to 6 locations and swipe between them. Home, office, the park where you run, your parents' place across the country — keep tabs on all of them.

STAY INFORMED WITHOUT OPENING THE APP
• Live Activity — when air quality deteriorates, Air Today appears on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island so you know at a glance.
• Widgets — small, medium, circular, rectangular, and inline. Put air quality on your Home Screen or Watch face in the format you prefer.

BEAUTIFUL BY DEFAULT
The atmospheric backgrounds aren't decorative. They're the interface. Metal shaders and particle systems create a living sky that responds to real conditions — clear skies shimmer, polluted air gets thick and heavy. You feel it before you read anything.

BUILT WITH RESPECT
Air Today is free. There are no ads. There is no tracking. There is no account to create. Your location data stays on your device and is used only to find nearby monitoring stations. That's it.

Air quality data is provided by the World Air Quality Index Project (waqi.info) and its global network of monitoring stations.

Made by one person who wanted a better way to decide whether to go for a run.
```
